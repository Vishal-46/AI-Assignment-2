Introduction

The PEAS framework helps define the capabilities and constraints of an AI agent. It stands for Performance Measure, Environment, Actuators, and Sensors, and provides a comprehensive way to understand how AI systems interact with their surroundings.

Performance Measure

The performance measure evaluates how well the AI agent achieves its goals. It can vary depending on the agent's application and objectives.

Environment

The environment is where the AI agent operates. It includes all external factors that influence the agent's behavior and performance.

Actuators

Actuators are the mechanisms through which the agent acts upon the environment. They can vary widely depending on the agent's design.

Sensors

Sensors allow the AI agent to perceive its environment, gather data, and make decisions based on the information received.
